About

I am currently pursuing a master's degree in Scientific Computing at the University of Cambridge, where I am focusing on atomistic simulation and advanced research techniques to prepare for a PhD in computational physics. Before Cambridge, I read Physics at the University of Bristol, graduating with first-class honors and receiving the Peter Fowler Memorial Prize for top academic performance. Beyond scientific research, I aim to communicate mathematics, science, and nature to inspire others to see the beauty in the world around us; motivating the creation of this website.
